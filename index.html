SS-Hybrid MVDR beamforming demo for the journal article

by Sina Hafezi

Scenario:

  • 6 sec chunks from SPEAR D1 (real-recording EasyCom) & D2 (simulated EasyCom)
  • Categorised based on the number of active sources in the chunk
  • Sample rate: 10kHz, STFT frame size: 16ms, STFT frame step: 8ms
  • Array microphones: Channels 1 to 6. Array reference channel: 2 (front-middle mic).

List of Methods:

  • Ref: Reference Signal (D1: time- & level-aligned close-talking headset mic, D2: anechoic target @ the array's reference channel)
  • Pass: Un-processed signal
  • Iso: Isotropic MVDR (Superdirective)
  • SSH: Subspace-Hybrid MVDR (Parametric Dictionary)
  • SSHK: Subspace-Hybrid MVDR (Data-Driven Dictionary)
